Rienzi



Rienzi, der Letzte der Tribunen is an opera by Richard Wagner, composed in five acts, with a libretto written by the composer after reading Edward Bulwer-Lytton's novel of the same title. Written between July 1838 and November 1840, it was released in Dresden on October 20, 1842."
