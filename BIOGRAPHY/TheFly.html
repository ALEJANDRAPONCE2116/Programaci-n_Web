The Flying Dutchman



The Flying Dutchman or The Ghost Ship is a romantic opera in three acts with music and libretto in German by Richard Wagner, inspired by Heinrich Heine's Memoirs of the Lord of Schnabelewopski. It premiered in Dresden, at the Königlich Sächsisches Hoftheater, on January 2, 1843.
